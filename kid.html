Children's Museum

An Exciting Experience for Kids at the Grand Egyptian Museum's Children's Museum

An experience designed to spark children's curiosity and bring them closer to their extraordinary cultural heritage through engaging, hands-on educational activities, perfectly designed for young inquisitive minds. With sensory stimulation, combining thrilling visuals, exciting sounds, and tangible materials, the museum becomes a magical, captivating place where young visitors can immerse themselves in the grandeur and mysteries of ancient Egypt, transforming history into a living and fascinating source of interest.

The Grand Egyptian Museum Offers Kids Tours Inside the "Children's Museum"

The Grand Egyptian Museum offers children's tours inside the “Children’s Museum” located within the museum. The place is designed to nurture kids' passion through customized educational experiences using visual, auditory, and sensory tools, with the main aim to captivate children and showcase the magnificence of ancient Egyptian civilization.

Tour Details by Age

We offer age-specific tours for children, with each tour designed to suit the needs of different age groups. Below are the details of the tours by age:

Children Aged 3 to 5

Interactive tours using colors and sounds to grab children's attention, with simple educational games explaining the ancient Egyptian civilization in a fun way.

Children Aged 6 to 8

Tours based on hands-on activities and interactive storytelling, allowing children to learn about the key aspects of ancient Egyptian civilization through play and engagement with exhibits.

Children Aged 9 to 12

Educational tours focused on an in-depth understanding of ancient Egypt's history and key discoveries, using multimedia techniques and group activities.

Children Aged 13 and Above

Advanced tours featuring detailed historical information and virtual reality experiences to enhance the learning experience.

More Details About Visiting the Children's Museum

The Children's Museum at the Grand Egyptian Museum is suitable for children aged 6-12.

The tour lasts 45 minutes.

The tour is available in both Arabic and English.

To book school visits, contact us at learning@gem.eg.

Non-Egyptian individuals married to Egyptians and their children, or vice versa, are eligible to purchase Egyptian entry tickets.

All bookings are non-transferable and non-refundable.

Parking is available on-site for a fee.
