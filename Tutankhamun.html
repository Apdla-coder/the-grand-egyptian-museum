Tutankhamun

Tutankhamun was an Egyptian pharaoh of the 18th dynasty, known for his intact tomb discovered in 1922...

He became king at a very young age and reigned for about 9 years. His reign helped restore traditional Egyptian religion after Akhenaten...

Although his reign was relatively short and modest, his fame skyrocketed due to the discovery of his well-preserved tomb filled with treasures...

The discovery by Howard Carter gave the world a rare glimpse into the wealth and artistry of ancient Egypt...

Tutankhamun's golden mask remains one of the most iconic symbols of ancient Egyptian civilization...
